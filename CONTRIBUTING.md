## How to Contribute

We welcome papers and articles about AI design patterns, algorithms, frameworks, and engineering approaches. Quality over quantity — every entry should offer actionable insights for real-world AI development.

### What We're Looking For

- Papers with novel design patterns or thinking approaches
- Production-proven optimization techniques (inference, training, deployment)
- Architecture designs that solve real engineering problems
- Framework comparisons with practical trade-off analysis

### What We're NOT Looking For

- Pure benchmark papers without design insight
- Incremental improvements without new thinking patterns
- Marketing content or product announcements

### Article Template

Each article should follow this structure:

```markdown
# Paper Title

> **Original link:** [arXiv:XXXX.XXXXX](https://arxiv.org/abs/XXXX.XXXXX)
>
> **Authors:** Author names
>
> **Published:** Year / Venue
>
> **Topic:** One-line description

---

## Abstract

[English original]

## 摘要

[Chinese translation]

---

## 1. Section Title / 章节标题

[English original]

[Chinese translation]

---

(Repeat for all major sections)
```

### Steps

1. Fork this repo
2. Create your article in the appropriate topic folder
3. If no matching folder exists, create one with a clear English name (e.g., `training-optimization/`)
4. Update `README.md` — add your paper to both the English and Chinese index tables
5. Submit a PR with a brief description of why this paper is worth reading

## 如何贡献

我们欢迎关于 AI 设计模式、算法、框架和工程方法的论文与文章。质量优先 — 每篇收录都应该为实际 AI 开发提供可落地的洞察。

### 我们在寻找什么

- 具有新颖设计模式或思维方式的论文
- 经过生产验证的优化技术（推理、训练、部署）
- 解决真实工程问题的架构设计
- 具有实际权衡分析的框架对比

### 我们不收录什么

- 没有设计洞察的纯跑分论文
- 没有新思维模式的增量改进
- 营销内容或产品公告

### 文章模板

每篇文章应遵循以下结构（见上方英文模板）。核心要求：
- 包含原文链接
- 英文原文与中文翻译逐段对照
- 按主题归入对应文件夹

### 步骤

1. Fork 本仓库
2. 在对应主题文件夹中创建文章
3. 若无匹配文件夹，用清晰的英文名新建（如 `training-optimization/`）
4. 更新 `README.md` — 在英文和中文索引表中都添加你的论文
5. 提交 PR，简述这篇论文为什么值得收录
